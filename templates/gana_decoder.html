Gaṇa Decoder

A gaṇa is a group of three Laghu-Guru units. Piṅgala uses gaṇas to describe Chandas patterns in a compact way.

This page identifies gaṇas using the traditional yamātārājabhānasalagam system.

In this app: Laghu = 1 Guru = 0
