Mātrā Meru / Mātrā Prastāra

Mātrā Meru counts how many Chandas patterns of a fixed total mātrā length contain exactly 0 Guru, 1 Guru, 2 Guru, and so on.

Back to Other Algorithms

This is similar to binomial coefficients, but here the counting is based on total mātrā value instead of fixed akṣara length.

Laghu = 1 mātrā Guru = 2 mātrās
